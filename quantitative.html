Quantitative Data Visualization

This visualization was created for the Data Visualization course given by Coursera with Prof. John C. Hart of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

The coordinated visualizations below (built with D3) show the top 10,000 most mentioned Twitter user names mentioned in sample of 100,000 english tweets taken from to off of the Twitter Sample Stream.

Some questions to consider: How does time-of-day correlate with arrival delay? Are longer or shorter names more likely to arrive early? What happened on January 12? How do name patterns differ between weekends and weekdays, or mornings and nights? Fork this example and try your own data!
