Non-Quantitative Visualization Dan Stadelman

This visualization was created for the Data Visualization course given by Coursera with Prof. John C. Hart of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

The coordinated visualizations below (built with dc.js using D3) show the top 10,000 Twitter names mentioned in a sample of 100,000 English tweets sampled off of the Twitter Sample Stream from Fri May 15 06:59:49 +0000 2015 to Fri May 22 14:29:45 +0000 2015.

How popular is the president compared to members of boy band One Direction? Did Taylor Swift do something interesting during the week(.)? What event was held on May 17(.)? There are also interesting differences between Twitter names mentioned during the day than in the wee hours of the morning.
